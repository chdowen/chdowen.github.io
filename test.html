Here's a collection of Madi's favourite wineries and both of our favourite restaurants. There are literally hundreds of other wineries and dozens of other restaurants, and they are also all very good.

This is just to narrow it down so y'all aren't all asking us for winery recommendations the week before the wedding.

Madi's Favourite Wineries

Schramsberg Vineyards

Historic hillside caves and exceptional sparkling wines. Perfect for a romantic toast with views of the valley.

Castello di Amorosa

A 13th-century Tuscan castle replica complete with torture chamber - delightfully gothic and mysterious.

Chateau Montelena

Famous for its Chardonnay that beat the French in the 1976 Paris tasting. Elegant gardens and lakeside setting.

Our Favourite Restaurants

The French Laundry

Thomas Keller's legendary fine dining establishment. Book well in advance for an unforgettable culinary experience.

Bouchon Bistro

Classic French bistro fare in an intimate setting. Perfect for a romantic dinner after wine tasting.

Oxbow Public Market

Artisanal food market with local vendors. Great for casual dining and discovering local specialties.

Things to Do

Hot Air Balloon Rides

Float above the vineyards at sunrise for breathtaking views of the valley. Several companies offer champagne flights.

Calistoga Spa Hot Springs

Relax in natural mineral pools and indulge in spa treatments. Perfect for unwinding after the festivities.

Silverado Trail

Scenic drive through the heart of wine country with stunning vineyard views and boutique winery stops.
